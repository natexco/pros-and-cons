Pros and Cons of Social media

Today, I will discuss the pros and cons of Social media, which will go over the good and bad parts of it.

Pros Cons
Information: Social media is the main source of real-time news that millions look at evey day to see what is happening either in their environment, town, city, state, and even country. False Information: Sometimes, not all information will be true, as there are some people online who spread false informatin for their own amusement.
Curiosity: Social meda can answer any questions that people might speculate. It is a good thing to have social media because of how eficient it is at answering questions. Distrations: Social media is usually found on devices that we use, such as phones, tablets, computers, etc. People tend to make it a big part of their daily life by staying on it too much.
Entertainment: This is the main purpose of social media, from all the funny videos, to the challenges,and the fails. 80% of the time, the average teenager would be on a social media app like Instagram or Tik Tok scrolling on their phone for entertainment. Mental health decline: Certain videos or topics can leave one's mind shattered. The brain is a fragile thing and there are some people on social media who can't handle everything that they see.
